LA Style Beef Short Rib

Description

This dish is a korean barbecue staple. A perfect blend of sweet and savory creates the best way to cook and eat beet short rib.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of beef short rib, cut like the picture
  • 8-10 cloves of garlic
  • half a medium sized onion, preferably yellow
  • 2 cups of soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp of black pepper
  • 1 half of an apple pear or korean pear
  • 3 tbps of brown sugar
  • To garnish: chop fresh green onions and sprinkle with roasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Add all the wet ingredients into a bowl and mix well
  2. In a food processor, finely mince all of the dry ingredients
  3. Once all the dry ingredients are minced, add them into a large bowl with the wet ingredient mixture
  4. Add the short rib to the mixture and make sure to coat all the short ribs very well. Let sit for at least 2 hours
  5. Once the marinade and the meat get to know each other, heat a medium sized non stick skillet to medium-high
  6. Place the meat into the hot pan with a little bit of oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan
  7. Cook each side in increments of 1 to 1 and a half minutes until the edges of the bone and meat look a little burnt
  8. Remove from heat and using some scissors cut the ribs into individual pieces and serve
